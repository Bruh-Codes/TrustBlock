# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want TrustBlock to be a respectful, technically serious, and inclusive project.

Participants are expected to:

- engage respectfully and in good faith
- give technical feedback directly, without hostility
- assume mistakes can be corrected through collaboration
- avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or intimidation

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- abusive or degrading language
- harassment or stalking
- doxxing or publishing private information
- trolling, bad-faith derailment, or repeated antagonistic behavior
- discriminatory conduct based on identity or background

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in:

- issues
- pull requests
- review threads
- project discussions
- other project-managed communication spaces

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately before escalating publicly.
